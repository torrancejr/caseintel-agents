SETTLEMENT AGREEMENT AND RELEASE

This Settlement Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2024, by and between:

PLAINTIFF: Robert Williams ("Williams")
DEFENDANT: Global Manufacturing Inc. ("Global")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Williams filed a lawsuit against Global in the Superior Court of California, Case No. 2023-CV-98765, alleging wrongful termination and age discrimination;

WHEREAS, the parties wish to resolve all disputes without further litigation;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SETTLEMENT PAYMENT
Global shall pay Williams the sum of ONE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND DOLLARS ($150,000.00) in full settlement of all claims. Payment shall be made within 30 days of execution of this Agreement.

2. RELEASE OF CLAIMS
Williams hereby releases and forever discharges Global, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from any and all claims, demands, and causes of action arising from or related to Williams' employment and termination.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
The parties agree that the terms of this Settlement Agreement shall remain strictly confidential. Neither party shall disclose the terms to any third party except as required by law or to their respective attorneys, accountants, or tax advisors.

4. NON-ADMISSION
This Agreement shall not be construed as an admission of liability or wrongdoing by Global. Global specifically denies all allegations of wrongful conduct.

5. DISMISSAL WITH PREJUDICE
Within 5 business days of receipt of the settlement payment, Williams shall file a Request for Dismissal with Prejudice of the lawsuit.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
