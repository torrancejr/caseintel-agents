EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Our Engineering team has completed a comprehensive safety analysis of Product Model X-500 following multiple customer complaints and two minor injury reports. This memo outlines critical safety defects that require immediate attention and corrective action.

FINDINGS

1. DEFECT IDENTIFICATION
   - Component failure rate: 12% higher than acceptable standards
   - Structural weakness in mounting bracket (Part #X500-MB-442)
   - Inadequate safety warnings in user manual
   - Design flaw allows unsafe operation under normal use conditions

2. RISK ASSESSMENT
   - Probability of serious injury: MODERATE TO HIGH
   - Estimated affected units: 45,000 units currently in market
   - Potential liability exposure: SIGNIFICANT

3. INCIDENT HISTORY
   - February 10, 2023: Customer complaint - bracket failure, minor injury
   - February 18, 2023: Customer complaint - product malfunction, property damage
   - February 24, 2023: Customer complaint - bracket failure, minor injury
   - March 1, 2023: Engineering testing confirms defect reproducibility

RECOMMENDATIONS

OPTION A: IMMEDIATE RECALL (RECOMMENDED)
- Issue voluntary recall within 7 days
- Estimated cost: $2.8 million
- Protects company reputation and limits liability
- Demonstrates commitment to customer safety

OPTION B: DELAYED RECALL (NOT RECOMMENDED)
- Delay recall until Q3 2023 (after quarterly earnings report)
- Estimated cost: $2.8 million + potential litigation costs
- Risk: Additional injuries during delay period
- Risk: Increased liability if delay becomes known

ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT POSITION

We strongly recommend OPTION A - immediate recall. The safety risk is real and documented. Any delay increases both the risk of serious injury and potential legal liability.

The cost of recall ($2.8M) is significantly less than potential litigation costs and reputational damage from continued incidents.

MANAGEMENT RESPONSE (Added March 15, 2023)

After review with Finance and Legal departments, Management has decided to implement OPTION B. We are aware of the potential safety issue but recommend delaying the recall until Q3 to avoid impacting quarterly earnings.

Rationale:
- Q1 earnings announcement scheduled for April 15, 2023
- Product Model X-500 represents 18% of Q1 revenue
- Immediate recall would require earnings restatement
- Board presentation scheduled for April 20, 2023

Action Items:
1. Monitor customer complaints closely
2. Prepare recall logistics for Q3 implementation
3. Increase product liability insurance coverage
4. Draft customer notification materials for future use
